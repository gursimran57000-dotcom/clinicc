Who We Are

Medicine meets
genuine care

At Dhukhbhanjan Clinic, we believe that healing is not just about prescriptions — it's about being heard, respected, and cared for as a whole person. Nestled in the heart of Bibi Wala, Bathinda, our clinic has become a trusted haven for families seeking compassionate medical guidance.

Our name itself — Dhukhbhanjan — means "reliever of sorrow." Every patient who walks through our doors is treated with warmth, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to their well-being.
